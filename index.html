Introduction

Is there an existing relationship between agricultural exposures and cancer?

In their article "Agricultural exposures and cancer," occupational cancer researchers Aaron Blair and Shelia H. Zahm write in their study that

"Farmers...appear to experience elevated rates for several cancers, including leukemia, non-Hodgkin's lymphoma, multiple myeloma, soft-tissue sarcoma, and cancers of the skin, lip, stomach, brain, and prostate.

However, other research has not found statistically significant evidence that shows agricultural exposures cause cancer.
